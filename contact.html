Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?
Depending on complexity, a design project typically takes 2-4 weeks, while a full development cycle ranges from 8-16 weeks.
What is your payment structure?
We usually operate on a 40/30/30 structure: 40% upfront deposit, 30% at midway milestone, and 30% upon final delivery.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Yes, we offer tiered maintenance retainers starting from 10 hours/month to ensure your system stays updated and secure.
Can you help with legacy system migration?
Absolutely. We specialize in refactoring legacy codebases into modern React/Node or Python architectures with zero data loss.
Is the security audit really free?
We provide a basic surface-level vulnerability scan for free to new clients. For deep-spectrum VAPT, we provide a custom quote.
